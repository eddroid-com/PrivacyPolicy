eddroid PurpleAir Companion Privacy Statement

This application does not collect any user data.

The following environment data is automatically included in Contact email messages only when sent by the user:
  • The application name, version, and build number.
  • The version of Android running on the device running this application.
  • The manufacturer and model of the device.
Email addresses from which the developer is contacted are saved to enable correspondance on whatever topic the user has initiated. By initiating contact the user agrees to receive email replies.

Email addresses and other information will never be sold or in any other way shared with any third party in the absence of legal requirements.
